About Us!

The CodeCloset is the perfect guide for beginners looking to code. We provide guides to common coding languages, as well as, more detailed support if you choose to contact us. Currently, the website is ran by the two of us, Kaydence Eng and Prince Manzano. You can see our lovely faces to the right of the screen. You can hover the image to learn who's who (the crown should be a dead giveaway ;) ). Our websites not only serves as a guide for our users, but as a journal for the two of us as to share our coding journey.

Our Story

We began this website when we first started learning how to code. Many resources exist out there to guide beginners in their coding journey, yet it was spread out everywhere. That's where we come in. We wanted to make one master site that anyone can refer to when they want to start coding. Helping others with their coding is key to our site's mission, however, we also want to educate people on how code came to the place it's at today. Hence, we also share knowledge regarding the history of code. Our site also provides information surrounding the current atmopshere of the coding world such as which languages are the most popular. Also, we found that a brief overview of the world of coding was beneficial when we first started, so we offer the same when users first visit our site. There are always people who are eager to code, but aren't too sure what it might entail, so we also provide guidance in that regard. The CodeCloset in your one stop place for all coding beginners out there!
